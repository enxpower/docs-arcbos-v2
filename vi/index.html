ARCBOS is a commercial autonomous outdoor robotics platform — not a single gadget. The first deployed category is industrial snow-removal robots, with the architecture engineered to scale across mowing, security, patrol, inspection and logistics in harsh environments.

The identity is built to earn the trust of municipal contractors, facility managers and heavy-equipment buyers: rugged, mechanical, reliable, and unmistakably built for work — not for the showroom.

CLEAR SPACE

Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the core aperture (×) on all sides. Keep free of competing graphics.

MINIMUM SIZE

24px digital min · 10mm print min

Below minimum size the tread row and sensor ticks lose legibility. Use the icon mark only at small scale.

MISUSE

  • Do not recolor the amber accent
  • Do not stretch or rotate the hex shell
  • Do not add gradients or glow effects
  • Do not place on busy photography without scrim
