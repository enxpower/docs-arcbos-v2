北美商业设施每年为除雪与融冰支付超过 US$225亿（2023） 的运维预算。这是已预算化、每年重复、采购流程完全标准化的需求（SIMA-10-2025 ANSI 国家标准，每年5月 RFP、9月1日授标）。问题从来不是"有没有需求"，而是：现有方案在极寒工况下太不可靠、太依赖人工夜班、太难规模化管理。
North American commercial facilities spend over US$22.5B (2023) annually on snow and ice management — budgeted, recurring, and fully standardized procurement (SIMA-10-2025 ANSI standard: RFP by May, awarded September 1). The problem has never been demand — it's that existing solutions are too unreliable in extreme cold, too dependent on overnight labor, and impossible to manage at scale.

机场自治除雪（Otto、Yeti）已证明极寒工况下无人化物理可行，但造价 >$200万/台，商业地产无法承受。消费级产品（Yarbo）止步于住宅吞吐量与 −25°C 边界。商业园区 · 数据中心 · 物流场站 这个区间——工业级需求、园区规模、可复制部署——目前没有成熟标准化产品。ARCBOS 正在填补这个空白。

Airport autonomous snow removal (Otto, Yeti) proves unmanned operation is physically viable in extreme cold — but costs >$2M/unit, making commercial real estate deployment impossible. Consumer products (Yarbo) stop at residential throughput and −25°C. The gap — commercial parks · data centers · logistics yards — industrial-grade demand, campus scale, repeatable deployment — has no mature standardized product. ARCBOS is filling this gap.

−30°C reliable cold start · heavy-load track traction (≥500kg) · unmanned decision-making · 12hr+ continuous operation. No existing commercial product covers all four simultaneously. This is an engineering barrier, not a patent barrier — replication requires real-world validation data, which is exactly what the angel round produces.

Is the CNY 45M pre-money valuation reasonable?
对标同阶段工业机器人项目（样机期、北美市场切入）估值区间通常为¥3,000–8,000万。¥4,500万处于合理中间位，不存在高估风险，也不贱卖创始人股权，为 Pre-A（通常¥1.5–3亿）留足上升空间。Pre-A 回报潜力 3–7倍。
Benchmark against comparable industrial robotics projects at prototype stage targeting North American markets: typical range CNY 30–80M. CNY 45M is a reasonable midpoint — no overvaluation risk, no undervaluing founder equity. Leaves significant upside for Pre-A (typically CNY 150–300M). Pre-A return potential: 3–7x.
烧钱速度受控吗？
Is the burn rate under control?
精干核心团队、轻资产场地、少管理层、重交付。月均约¥74万，前高后低，符合硬件项目规律。6–8个月周期，无过长研发黑盒。无豪华办公室，无管理层溢价，无大规模招聘——钱都在产品上。
Lean core team, asset-light facilities, minimal management overhead, delivery-focused. ~¥740K/month average, front-loaded per hardware project norms. 6–8 month cycle. No lavish offices, no management premium, no large-scale hiring — capital goes to the product.
技术路线务实吗？
Is the technical approach pragmatic?
低速自治、Teach & Repeat、远程接管路线，不做L4/L5短期承诺。务实可执行——先跑通，再进化。算法架构留有世界模型扩展入口，但不以此承诺Alpha阶段交付。这是工业机器人最合理的MVP技术选型。
Low-speed autonomy, Teach & Repeat, remote teleoperation — no L4/L5 short-term promises. Pragmatic and executable — run first, then evolve. Algorithm architecture has world model expansion hooks, but these are not committed to Alpha-stage delivery. The most rational MVP technical approach for industrial robotics.
