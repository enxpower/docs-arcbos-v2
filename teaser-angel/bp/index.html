北美商业设施每年为除雪与融冰支付超过 US$225亿（2023） 的运维预算。这不是新需求，不需要教育市场——它已经存在、每年重复、采购流程完全标准化（SIMA-10-2025 ANSI 国家标准，每年5月 RFP、9月1日授标）。问题从来不是"有没有需求"，而是：现有方案在极寒工况下太不可靠、太依赖人工夜班、太难规模化管理。
North American commercial facilities spend over US$22.5B (2023) annually on snow and ice management. This is not a new category — it's budgeted, recurring, and fully standardized (SIMA-10-2025 ANSI standard: RFP by May, awarded September 1). The problem has never been demand — it's that existing solutions are too unreliable in extreme cold, too dependent on overnight labor, and impossible to manage at scale.

机场自治除雪（Otto、Yeti）已证明极寒工况下无人化物理可行；消费级产品（Yarbo）止步于住宅吞吐量与 −25°C 边界。商业园区 · 数据中心 · 物流场站 这个区间——工业级需求、园区规模、可复制部署——目前没有成熟的标准化产品。ARCBOS 正在填补这个空白。

Airport autonomous snow removal (Otto, Yeti) has proven unmanned operation is physically viable in extreme cold. Consumer products (Yarbo) stop at residential throughput and −25°C. The gap — commercial parks · data centers · logistics yards — industrial-grade demand, campus scale, repeatable deployment — has no mature standardized product. ARCBOS is filling this gap.
