北美商业设施每年为除雪与融冰支付超过 US$225亿（2023） 的运维预算。这不是新需求，不需要教育市场——它已经存在、每年重复、采购流程完全标准化（SIMA-10-2025 ANSI 国家标准，5月 RFP、9月1日授标）。问题从来不是"有没有需求"，而是：现有方案在极寒工况下太不可靠、太难管理、太依赖人工夜班。

机场自治除雪（Otto、Yeti）已证明极寒工况下无人化物理可行；消费级产品（Yarbo）止步于住宅吞吐量与−25°C 边界。商业园区 · 数据中心 · 物流场站这个区间——工业级需求、园区规模、可复制部署——目前没有成熟的标准化产品。ARCBOS 正在填补这个空白。
North American commercial facilities spend over US$22.5B (2023) annually on snow and ice management. This is not a new category — it's budgeted, recurring, and fully standardized (SIMA-10-2025 ANSI standard: RFP by May, awarded September 1). The problem has never been demand — it's that existing solutions fail in extreme cold, are unmanageable at night, and collapse under labor scarcity.

Airport autonomous snow removal (Otto, Yeti) has proven unmanned operation is physically viable in extreme cold. Consumer products (Yarbo) stop at residential throughput and −25°C. The gap — commercial parks · data centers · logistics yards — industrial-grade demand, campus scale, repeatable deployment — has no mature standardized product today. ARCBOS is filling this gap.

天使轮定位与目的
Angel Round Purpose
天使轮的逻辑非常直接：这笔钱用来做一件事——把工程样机做出来，进雪地验证，拿到真实测试数据。融资目标 ¥500–600万人民币，基准预算 ¥590万，含 11% 风险储备。

本轮完成后，ARCBOS 将持有三项 Pre-A 融资的核心资产：可运行的 Alpha/Beta 样机（从图纸到实体）、雪地/低温真实测试视频（最有说服力的融资材料）、基础自治演示能力（Teach & Repeat + 远程接管 + 基础避障）。结合已确认的试点意向方，Pre-A/A 轮融资讨论的技术基础已具备。
The angel round logic is straightforward: this capital does one thing — build the engineering prototype, enter real snow-field validation, and produce real test data. Fundraising target: ¥5–6M RMB, baseline budget: ¥5.9M, including 11% risk reserve.

Upon completion, ARCBOS will hold three core assets for Pre-A fundraising: a running Alpha/Beta prototype (from drawing to physical system), real snow/cold-field test video (the most compelling fundraising asset), and basic autonomy demonstration capability (Teach & Repeat + remote takeover + basic obstacle avoidance). Combined with confirmed pilot intent, the technical foundation for Pre-A/Series A conversations is complete.
