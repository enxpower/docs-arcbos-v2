北美商业设施除雪市场 US$225亿（2023），已预算化、每年重复，采购流程完全标准化（SIMA 5月 RFP · 9月授标）。天使轮目标只有一个：用¥400–600万人民币完成工程样机 Alpha/Beta，进入冬季真实工况验证，产出雪地测试视频与基础自治演示，以此支撑 Pre-A/A 轮融资讨论。预算基准 ¥590万，月均约74万，含 11% 风险储备。钱用在底盘、作业机构、测试、安全——不是团队扩张，不是豪华办公。当前关键零件已到货，样机进入组装阶段；CBRE、Colliers 渠道内 50+ 大型商业设施负责人已明确试点意向。

North American commercial snow management is a US$22.5B (2023) budgeted, recurring market with fully standardized procurement (SIMA May RFP · September award). The Angel Round has one goal: ¥4–6M RMB to complete Alpha/Beta engineering prototype, enter winter field validation, and produce snow-field test video and basic autonomy demo — the assets that directly unlock Pre-A/Series A fundraising. Budget baseline: ¥5.9M, ~¥740K/month avg, including 11% risk reserve. Capital goes to chassis, work mechanism, testing, and safety — not headcount or offices. Core components have arrived; prototype is in assembly. 50+ large facility managers within CBRE and Colliers networks have confirmed pilot intent.
