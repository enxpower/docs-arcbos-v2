北美商业设施除雪与融冰服务市场规模 US$225亿（2023），高度分散、采购标准化、每年重复——这是已预算化的刚性运维支出。关键零件已到货，工程样机进入组装阶段；CBRE、Colliers 管理体系内的设施负责人已明确表达冬季试点意向。
缺的不是设备，而是一套可在 −30°C、无人值守、12小时连续运行、产出可审计 LOS 记录的工业级自治系统。SnowBot 正在填补这个结构性空白。

The North American commercial snow & ice management market is US$22.5B (2023) — budgeted, recurring, compliance-driven. Not a new category. An existing spend waiting to be automated. Core components have arrived and prototype assembly is underway; facility managers within the CBRE and Colliers networks have confirmed winter pilot interest.
The gap is not equipment. It's a system that works at −30°C, unmanned, for 12+ hours straight, with auditable LOS records. SnowBot is filling that structural gap.
