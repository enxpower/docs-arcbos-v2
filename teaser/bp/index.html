北美商业设施的冬季除雪，每年消耗超过 US$225亿的运维预算。这不是新需求，不需要教育市场——它已经存在、每年重复、采购流程完全标准化（SIMA-10-2025，5月 RFP、9月授标）。问题从来不是"有没有需求"，而是：现有方案在极寒工况下太不可靠、太难管理、太依赖人工夜班。

North American commercial facilities spend over US$22.5B annually on snow and ice management. This is not a new category — it's a budgeted, recurring, compliance-driven operational expense. The procurement process is fully standardized (SIMA-10-2025: RFP by May, awarded by September 1). The problem has never been demand — it's that existing solutions fail in extreme cold, are unmanageable at night, and collapse under labor scarcity.

机场自治除雪（Otto、Yeti）已证明极寒工况下无人化物理可行；消费级产品（Yarbo）止步于住宅吞吐量与 −25°C 边界。商业园区 · 数据中心 · 物流场站这个区间——工业级需求、园区规模、可复制部署——目前没有一个成熟的标准化产品。

Airport autonomous snow removal (Otto, Yeti) has proven unmanned operation is physically viable in extreme cold. Consumer products (Yarbo) stop at residential throughput and −25°C. The gap — Commercial parks · Data centers · Logistics yards — industrial-grade demand, campus scale, repeatable deployment — has no mature standardized product today.

ARCBOS 正在填补这个空白。SnowBot 的关键硬件选型已完成、核心零件已到货，工程样机进入组装阶段。与此同时，创始人通过多年北美 C&I 能源客户网络，已从 CBRE、Colliers 管理体系内的设施负责人获得明确的试点意向确认。本轮融资的目标只有一个：用$200万美元把样机做出来，进入冬季验证，拿下首批付费试点。

ARCBOS is filling this gap. Key hardware selection is complete, core components have arrived, and the engineering prototype is in assembly. Through the founder's North American C&I energy client network, facility managers within the CBRE and Colliers ecosystem have confirmed pilot interest in writing and in meetings. This round has one goal: $2M to finish the prototype, complete winter validation, and close the first paid pilots.
