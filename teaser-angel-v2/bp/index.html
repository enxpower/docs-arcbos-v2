Problem & Market

TAM · SAM · SOM · SIMA CALENDAR
CUSTOMER PAIN · 01

凌晨夜间除雪：高失败成本 + 无法审计

Overnight Clearance: High Failure Cost + Zero Audit Trail

数据中心、物流仓储、医疗院区须在凌晨2–6时完成关键区域清雪。现有方案依赖临时司机与分包网络，在大雪天气恰好最不可靠。一次失误 = 运营中断 + 滑倒诉讼，无可核查记录。

Data centers, logistics parks, and medical campuses must clear critical zones by 2–6 AM. Current solutions rely on temp drivers and subcontractors — least reliable exactly when storms hit. One failure = operational shutdown + slip-and-fall litigation. No verifiable audit trail.

CUSTOMER PAIN · 02

外包成本持续通胀 · 劳动力短缺加剧

Outsource Cost Inflation · Worsening Labor Shortage

北美商业除雪服务人工+设备典型年费 US$40,000–60,000/站，持续通胀。SIMA研究显示从业司机年缺口超过8%，冬季劳动力供给结构性恶化。机器人是设施管理人员实际寻找的解决方案，而不是技术噱头。

Typical commercial snow management outsourcing: US$40,000–60,000/site/year, with persistent cost inflation. SIMA research shows annual operator shortage exceeding 8%. Winter labor supply structurally worsening. Robotics is what facility managers are actively looking for — not a technology gimmick.

TECH CHOICE 01 · No L4/L5

Teach & Repeat · 不承诺 L4/L5

操作员录制路线一次，机器人反复执行，遇障切换远程接管。这是工业移动机器人MVP阶段最可靠的技术选型，在仓储自动化、机场地勤、设施清洁等场景已大量验证，避免在雪地低纹理环境中押注未验证的SLAM技术。

Operator records route once; robot repeats autonomously with remote override on anomalies. Most reliable MVP tech choice for industrial mobile robots — widely validated in warehouse automation, ground support, and facility cleaning. Avoids betting on unproven SLAM in low-texture snow environments.

Core software stack on ROS2 open-source (chassis control, path execution, sensor fusion, OTA) — no reinventing wheels. Snow SLAM and low-texture adaptation handled by external algorithm advisors (included in ¥350K software budget), minimizing full-time engineering headcount.

Chassis, motors/reducers, battery/BMS, and industrial PCs sourced directly from Chinese industrial supply chain — 40–60% lower cost vs. equivalent NA sourcing. Enables reasonable gross margin within RaaS pricing constraints. Co-founder Gong Jifeng manages supply chain.

安全合规路径

Safety & Compliance Roadmap

OSHA · UL 3100 · FUNCTIONAL SAFETY · INSURANCE
WHY SAFETY IS CHAPTER 05 — NOT A FOOTNOTE

移动机器人进入商业设施后，安全不是可选功能，是商业准入前提。OSHA 29 CFR 1910.217 等标准将移动机器人碰撞、挤压、意外运动列为重要风险；设施管理方在签署任何试点协议前都会要求安全证明文件。本章节描述ARCBOS的系统性安全方案，而不是将认证推迟到未来。

For mobile robots entering commercial facilities, safety is not optional — it is the commercial admission prerequisite. OSHA 29 CFR 1910.217 and related standards flag mobile robot collisions, entrapment, and unintended motion as significant risks. Facility managers require safety documentation before signing any pilot agreement. This chapter describes ARCBOS's systematic safety approach — not deferred certification.

FUNCTIONAL SAFETY · HARDWARE

双通道急停 + 通信丢失保护

Dual-Channel E-Stop + Comms Loss Protection

双通道独立急停回路（物理按钮 + 软件指令），任一通道触发立即制动；通信信号丢失3秒自动停车；操作区域内视觉+声音双重警示。这是商业设施试点的最低安全要求，Beta B3阶段完成实现并测试。

Dual-channel independent e-stop circuit (physical button + software command) — either triggers immediate braking. Comms signal loss triggers auto-stop within 3 seconds. Visual and audible warning systems in operation zone. Minimum commercial facility requirement — implemented and tested at Beta B3.

FUNCTIONAL SAFETY · DETECTION

人员检测 + 安全区域逻辑

Personnel Detection + Safety Zone Logic

多传感器融合（超声波+摄像头）实现一定范围内人员/障碍物检测；检测到人员进入安全区域时自动减速或停止；作业时间通常为凌晨人流稀少期，降低交互风险。Beta B3验收标准：2米内检测并停止。

Multi-sensor fusion (ultrasonic + camera) for personnel/obstacle detection. Auto-decelerate or stop when personnel enter safety zone. Operations scheduled during low-traffic overnight hours to minimize interaction risk. Beta B3 acceptance: detect and stop within 2m.

Clearly defined: accident liability in autonomous mode (ARCBOS/insurance) vs. during remote operator intervention (operator + ARCBOS) vs. facility safety zone management (client). All three must be contractually defined in first pilot agreements — no ambiguity.

战略并购

Strategic Acquisition

Outdoor equipment platforms (e.g. Toro, Husqvarna, Briggs & Stratton portfolio brands) or facility management SaaS platforms are the most likely strategic acquirers. Precedent: Toro acquired Left Hand Robotics for ~$40M in 2021, proving large outdoor equipment companies will acquire autonomous robotics capabilities. ARCBOS value: NA-validated extreme-cold data, commercial customer base, RaaS ops system, and Chinese supply chain advantage. Acquisition could occur between Pre-A and A+ stage — not guaranteed.
