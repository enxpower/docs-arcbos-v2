01

问题：北美大型设施的夜间除雪困境

THE PROBLEM

CUSTOMER PAIN FIRST
Pain 01 · Reliability

夜间除雪 · 依赖人工 · 失败成本极高

Night Clearance Depends on Human Labor

数据中心、物流仓储、医疗院区必须在凌晨2–6时完成入口清雪。现有方案依赖临时司机与分包网络，大雪时恰好最不可靠。一次失误意味着运营中断与法律责任。

Data centers, logistics parks, and medical campuses must clear entry points by 2–6 AM. Current solutions rely on temp drivers and subcontractors — least reliable exactly during storms. One failure means operational shutdown and liability exposure.

Pain 02 · Auditability

无法审计的服务结果 · 责任闭环缺失

Zero Auditability · No Liability Closure

企业客户无法核查实际除雪时间、路线覆盖和作业质量。滑倒事故诉讼的核心争议"有没有按时清扫"——目前这个问题没有可信答案。

Facility managers cannot verify actual clearing times, route coverage, or quality. Slip-and-fall litigation hinges on "was it cleared on time?" — currently an unanswerable question with no credible audit trail.

机场级自主除雪（Otto/Yeti）已证明极寒工况下无人化在工程上成立，但造价超过 $200万/台，商业地产完全无法承受。消费级产品（Yarbo，~$5,000，官方额定−25°C）止步于住宅吞吐量。商业园区 · 数据中心 · 物流场站——这个区间，目前没有经过真实极寒工况验证的成熟产品。ARCBOS SnowBot 填补这个空白。

Airport-grade snow removal (Otto/Yeti) proves unmanned extreme-cold operation is viable — but at over $2M/unit, commercial real estate deployment is impossible. Consumer products (Yarbo, ~$5,000, rated −25°C) stop at residential throughput. Commercial parks · data centers · logistics yards — no mature extreme-cold-validated product exists for this tier. ARCBOS SnowBot fills this gap.

APPROACH 01 · No L4/L5

天使轮不承诺 L4/L5 自主。选用 Teach & Repeat + 远程接管：操作员录制路线，机器人反复执行，遇障切换远程干预。工业移动机器人最务实的 MVP 选型。

No L4/L5 promises. Teach & Repeat + remote teleoperation: operator records route once, robot repeats; anomalies trigger remote takeover. Most pragmatic MVP for industrial mobile robots.

APPROACH 02 · 4-Axis Barrier

任何单项可被复制；−30°C × 重载履带 × 雪地低纹理导航 × 夜间无人值守四项同时工程验证，需要完整冬季真实数据积累——这正是本轮融资要产出的东西。

−30°C × heavy-track × low-texture snow nav × unmanned night ops simultaneously validated requires real winter field data — exactly what this round funds.

APPROACH 03 · SW Budget ¥35万

底层基于 ROS2 开源框架，无框架授权费。算法顾问（外聘）承担 SLAM 难题与雪地适配。¥35万覆盖集成、定制化与顾问费。

Stack built on ROS2 open-source (no licensing fee). Algorithm advisors handle SLAM and snow adaptation. ¥350K covers integration, customization, and advisor fees.
