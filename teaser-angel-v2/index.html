01

问题：北美大型设施的夜间除雪困境

THE PROBLEM

CUSTOMER PAIN FIRST
Pain 01 · Reliability

夜间除雪 · 依赖人工 · 失败成本极高

Night Clearance Depends on Human Labor

数据中心、物流仓储园区、医疗院区必须在凌晨2–6时完成入口清雪。现有方案依赖临时司机、分包网络，每逢大雪恰好是司机最紧缺的时候。一次失误意味着运营中断与法律责任。

Data centers, logistics parks, and medical campuses must clear entry points by 2–6 AM. Existing solutions rely on temp drivers and subcontractors — exactly when labor is least available during a storm. One failure means operational shutdown and liability exposure.

Pain 02 · Auditability

无法审计的服务结果 · 责任闭环缺失

Zero Auditability · No Liability Closure

企业客户（FM/物业经理）无法核查实际除雪时间、路线覆盖和作业质量。滑倒事故诉讼的核心争议往往是"有没有按时清扫"——目前这个问题没有可信答案。

Facility managers cannot verify actual clearing times, route coverage, or quality. Slip-and-fall litigation hinges on "was it cleared on time?" — currently an unanswerable question with no credible audit trail.

机场级自主除雪（Otto/Yeti）已证明极寒工况下无人化在工程上成立，但造价超过 $200万/台，商业地产完全无法承受。消费级产品（Yarbo，~$5,000，额定−25°C）止步于住宅吞吐量与设施安全要求。商业园区 · 数据中心 · 物流场站——这个区间，目前没有经过真实极寒工况验证的成熟产品。ARCBOS SnowBot 填补这个空白。

Airport-grade autonomous snow removal (Otto/Yeti) proves unmanned extreme-cold operation is physically viable — but at over $2M/unit, commercial real estate deployment is impossible. Consumer products (Yarbo, ~$5,000, rated −25°C) stop at residential throughput and fall short of facility safety requirements. Commercial parks · data centers · logistics yards — this tier has no mature, extreme-cold-validated product. ARCBOS SnowBot fills this gap.

APPROACH 01 · No L4/L5 Promise

天使轮阶段不承诺 L4/L5 自主。选用 Teach & Repeat + 远程接管 路线：操作员录制路线，机器人按路线重复执行；遇障或异常切换远程干预。这是工业移动机器人最务实的 MVP 技术选型，已在仓储、机场地勤等场景大量验证。

No L4/L5 promises for the angel stage. Teach & Repeat + remote teleoperation: operator records route once, robot repeats autonomously; anomalies trigger remote takeover. The most pragmatic MVP approach for industrial mobile robotics, widely validated in warehouse and ground-support applications.

APPROACH 02 · Engineering Barrier = 4-Axis

任何单项指标都可被复制；−30°C 可靠性 × 重载履带牵引 × 雪地低纹理导航 × 无人值守夜间作业 四项同时工程验证，需要完整冬季真实数据积累——而这正是本轮融资要产出的东西。

Any single capability can be copied. −30°C reliability × heavy-track traction × low-texture snow navigation × unmanned night operation simultaneously validated requires real winter field data — which is exactly what this round funds.

APPROACH 03 · Software ¥350K — How

软件预算 ¥35万：底层基于 ROS2 开源框架，覆盖底盘控制、路径执行、OTA；算法顾问（外聘）承担 SLAM 难题与雪地环境适配。¥35万仅覆盖集成与定制化开发，不包含框架本身成本。

SW budget ¥350K: stack built on ROS2 open-source (chassis control, path execution, OTA); algorithm advisors (external) handle SLAM challenges and snow-environment adaptation. ¥350K covers integration and customization — framework itself is open-source.
